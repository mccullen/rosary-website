{{ whyWasTheRosaryGiven.text }}

Almighty God has given priests the Rosary because he wants them to use it as a means to convert the most hardened sinners and most obstinate heritics.

{{ howPriestsSpreadDevotion.text }}

Priests must spread devotion to the Rosary by:

  1. Practicing it (saying it every day)
  2. Preaching it (encourging others to say it)

{{ howMuchGoodFromPreaching.text }}

God and his blessed Mother will pour abundant grace into his soul, so that he may become God's instrument for his glory; and his word, though simple, will do more good in one month than that of other preachers in several years.

St. Louis-Marie de Montfort, The Admirable Secret of the Rosary, A White Rose

{{ whyPreachingIsntEnough.text }}

Merely preaching isn't enough because people cannot be expected to act on your advice if you do not practice it. No one can give what he does not have: "Jesus began to do and to teach." (Acts 1:1)
